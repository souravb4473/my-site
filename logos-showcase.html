Version 1 — Mark + Wordmark

Focus-bracket icon references the viewfinder without being literal. Works as a standalone icon at small sizes (favicons, profile photos).

  • Use mark alone at 32px or smaller
  • Horizontal layout for website nav
  • Stacked layout (mark above, wordmark below) for social avatars

Version 2 — Typographic

Cormorant italic gives the full name personality without a mark. The rule-and-counter-rule detail creates editorial structure. Distinctive, scalable.

  • Best at large sizes — hero, print, watermark
  • Works well as a portfolio watermark on images
  • Pair with Version 1 mark for a complete brand system
