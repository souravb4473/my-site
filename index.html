About

Why I shoot.

I've spent my career in boardrooms and airports — Detroit, Mumbai, Singapore, London. You move that much, you either stop noticing the world or you start paying attention to it differently.

I chose the second one.

The camera came later. But the eye was always there.

What I shoot is shaped by everything I've chased outside of work:

  • Roads on a motorcycle, where you notice things at 60 mph that a car never shows you
  • Meals in cities where I didn't speak the language but understood the plate
  • Moments in places I knew I wouldn't return to

That's what I bring to every shoot. Not a formula. An eye trained by genuine curiosity.

Based in Troy, MI. I shoot locally and travel for the right work.
